Descriptive Description:
The world's oldest 100-mile trail ride, established in 1955. Ride from Tahoe to Auburn in one day.

Directions:
Start is at Robie Park near Truckee, CA. Take I-80 to the Truckee exit, then follow Highway 267 north. Turn right onto Northwoods Blvd, then right onto Fairway Drive to Robie Park.
